Four Strategic Pillars

Upgrading our standards to unlock the full power of our new operating system

Migrate Value, Not Noise

Moving clutter creates new mess. This migration is a forcing function to sanitize data.

The Requirement: Teams must audit and map their current Monday boards and Confluence pages.

If a process cannot be documented clearly, it will not be migrated.

Clarity Enables Automation

AI agents cannot summarize ambiguity. Clean inputs create automated value.

The Requirement: Define exactly what "done" means before syncing Jira to Notion.

Without clear DoD, syncs create noise; with it, they create value.

Open by Default

Building a shared brain where knowledge flows freely across teams.

The Requirement: Work-related pages belong in Team Space, not private pages.

Sensitive data protected via permissions; everything else defaults to visibility.

Structure Powers the AI

Standardization allows AI agents to read and act on your data.

The Requirement: Use standardized project and knowledge base templates.

Breaking structure breaks AI. Customize content, not database structure.

Action Required

To initiate Wave 1, Team Leads must complete these immediate steps:

  • 1. Appoint a Notion Champion
    Choose one person to co-build the workspace with Edith
  • 2. Audit Your Data
    Delete old/irrelevant Monday items. Rule: We migrate value, not noise
  • 3. List Top 3 Pain Points
    Example: "I spend 2 hours on manual updates." We'll prioritize automating these first
  • 4. Blueprint Your Ideal Workflow
    Think beyond tool constraints. How would you perfectly manage a project with zero limitations?
